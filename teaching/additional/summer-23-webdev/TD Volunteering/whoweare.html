We have helped over 100,000 students through their volunteering process, and currently work with over 1,000 schools to ensure that their students are able to get the aid that they need. We work with over 1,000 organizations throughout the country, each varying from state to state. These volunteering opportunities range from gardening and food banks to sports. Through our program we ensure that students volunteer for organizations that are suiting for them.

Unity Gardens is a non-profit organization that aids students with their knowledge of plants and nature. They provide volunteering services to students who are interested in furturing their involvement within the community, specifically by aiding with the maintance of the garden, which provides free and healthy food in low income neighbourhoods.

Unity Gardens

TD Volunteering works with a variety of food banks, but one of the food banks we work alongside is the foodbank in Northern Indiana. Foodbanks allow students to give back to the community. Volunteering for the foodbank often involves restocking canned foods, organizing these foods, and distributing these foods to families who are in need of aid.

TD Volunteering is a proud supporter of the Boys and Girls Club. Through TD Volunteering we help students get connected and aid them through the process of becoming a volunteering for this extremely beneficial club. The Boys and Girls Club is a club that aids with the development of children of all ages.
