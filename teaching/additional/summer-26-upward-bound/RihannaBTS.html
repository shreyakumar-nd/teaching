    Life before Debut

    BTS started off as just normal teens from South Korea. RM (Kim Namjoon) was a well-known underground rapper known as “Runch Randa”. Jin (Kim Seokjin) didn’t have a musical background, unlike everyone else; he was a university acting student when he was scouted by an agent because of his good looks. Suga (Min Yoongi), originally from Daegu, was also an underground rapper known as “Gloss”. Suga agreed to join only on the condition that he didn’t have to dance, but Big Hit lied to him; he ended up staying because he enjoyed it.

    J-Hope (Jung Hoseok) was an underground dancer in Gwangju who was initially auditioning for JYP Entertainment before being scouted by Big Hit. Jimin (Park Jimin) was a contemporary dancer from Busan that the agents from Big Hit thought was very good. V (Kim Taehyung) tagged along with a friend for the audition, but the staff urged him to audition as well. Jungkook (Jeon Jungkook) auditioned for the talent show Superstar K and was scouted by seven different companies, but he ultimately chose Big Hit because he thought RM was really cool.

    Debut

    The group debuted on June 13, 2013, known as BTS. They were very broke and could barely afford food, and they all slept in one room. Life was very hard for them, and they even got lots of criticism from people, yet they never gave up. They soon started becoming famous in 2015 after their hit “I Need You”. After that hit they just became even bigger and bigger. At one point the pressure was very hard in 2018 that they were thinking about disbanding but they chose to stick together.

    In 2022, they had to disband due to military service, and it was very sad, but in 2025 they came back bigger and stronger, and on March 20, 2026, they came out with their newest album called Arirang, which included their single, Swim, and 14 songs.
