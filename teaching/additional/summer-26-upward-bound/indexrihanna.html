    finding BTS

    When I was 8, I remember being at my cousin’s house because it was her birthday, and I went into her room and asked about her posters, and she said it was K-pop and played some music for me. It was BTS; I thought they sounded really cool, and I started listening to them. I didn’t listen to them as much, but as I got older, that changed.

    Life with them

    Their newer music was very good, but I mainly liked listening to their older music from 2014 and 2015. I enjoyed listening to their rap, and it made me feel excited. It didn’t matter where I was; my room, my aunt's house, or even out shopping.
