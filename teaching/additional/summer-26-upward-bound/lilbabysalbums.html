    Introduction

    Lil Baby’s discography includes four official studio albums, one collaborative studio project, and various projects across streaming platforms. His latest studio release is WHAM (Extended Version) (2025), alongside his 2022 release It's Only Me, his 2020 multi-platinum project My Turn, and collaborative albums.

    His main studio albums include Harder Than Ever (2018), My Turn (2020), and It's Only Me (2022)

    • Harder Than Ever
      His debut studio album, featuring the hit song "Yes Indeed" with Drake.
    • My Turn
      The massively successful, 4x Platinum sophomore release that included hit singles like "Emotionally Scarred" and "Sum 2 Prove".
    • Its Only Me
      His third solo LP, reaching #1 on the Billboard 200 and featuring tracks like "Heyy" and "In a Minute".
